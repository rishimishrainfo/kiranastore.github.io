Our Story

Three decades of serving the community with love and trust

28+ Years of
Excellence

Preserving Tradition, Embracing Quality

Founded by Shrimati Amravati Mishra in 1980, Mishra Kirana Store has been a cornerstone of the community, providing fresh, authentic Indian groceries and household essentials. What started as a small 200 sq ft shop has now grown into a trusted name for quality products.

We take pride in sourcing the finest grains, pulses, spices, and fresh produce directly from farmers and trusted suppliers. Our commitment to fair pricing and genuine products has earned us the love and trust of thousands of families over the generations.
